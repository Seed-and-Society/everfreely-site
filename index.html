Most people can't afford any of these. And even those who can are still missing thousands of smaller opportunities that add up to significant capital over time.

EverFreely™ replaces all three for a flat monthly fee, runs continuously, and applies at a volume no human operation can match.

How it works

The system
handles the pursuit.

01

Profile once

Complete a one-time setup. Business profile. Student profile if applicable. A library of your own pre-written materials that the agent draws from. Twenty minutes. Never repeated.

02

Agent discovers

Every day the agent searches curated sources across grants, scholarships, fellowships, pitch competitions, sponsorships, and more. Scored against your exact profile.

03

Drafts assemble

For every opportunity that clears your threshold, a complete draft application assembles from your asset library. Your voice. Your facts. Never fabricated.

04

You review. You submit.

Approve, edit, or skip. Everything submission-ready with a direct link to the application portal and a checklist of supporting documents — submitted with a single click. Nothing goes out without your sign-off.
